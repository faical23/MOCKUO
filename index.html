The world’s first dna-based COVID-19 diagnostic suitable for home use.

nanoSPLASH is an oral COVID-19 diagnostic Test that works in minutes. Spit into the bottle and your saliva will change colors to indicate if you have recently been infected with COVID-19.

1 . Spit into the tube and wait 10 minutes

2 . Unscrew the bottom cap and twist it on the top

3 . Wait 30 minutes for the liquid to either change color or remain the same
